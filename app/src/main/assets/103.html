  • How it works
    • THROUGH APP
      • Download the Dineout app from dineout.co.in/get
      • Go to You > Promotions & Gift Card in the app; enter your 16-digit gift card number and the 6-digit PIN
      • Book a table at any smartPay restaurant in our app
      • When you ask for the bill, tap on “Pay Now” on the home screen, enter the amount, selecting the “Use Dineout Earnings” checkbox.
    • THROUGH MOBILE SITE / WEBSITE OR THROUGH REQUESTING AT OUR CONCIERGE (Platforms other than APP)
      • Booking is done through mobile site/ website or by calling at our concierge (+91 9212340202)
      • Post booking the table he/ she(diner) will receive a confirmation sms & another sms with the payment link;
      • Post dining & once the user receives the bill, he/ she would be able to apply the voucher code & pin & pay the bill using the link;
      • A confirmation sms would be received by the user after making the payment to the restaurant.
